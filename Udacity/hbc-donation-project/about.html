College Student Crisis

College students are facing a very hard time in college on the finacial side of things. We have students working 40+ hours are week and still aren't making ends meet. How is a student suppose to work fulltime plus overtime and find time to study and get adequet amount of sleep? They can't and on top of that most college students face these three problems when attending to college:

Low on Funds

College students are more prone to being broke than normal people. They have a lot of expenses and have very little money.

No Food

College students are on such a tight budget there is sometimes no money left for food. Those who are are meal plans often find themselves running out of food.

Expensive Books

Textbooks cost a lot of money even the used ones cost a fortune. Sometimes these books are needed for the first week but students can't afford it So they end up falling behind on material.

This is where YOU come in!

We are a small not-for-profit company so we are soley based on donation. We consist of a small team of 4 who reach travel across the country to difference college campuses to see the faces in need of funding .With just a small donation you could help combat this finacial crisis for students. You'd be paying for someones meals, their books, school supplies, and much more. This is a tax deductable donation and is going to a good cause. If that isn't enough incentive then remember back to the time when you were really broke. Would you have wished to have a fund like this around? If so Click here to donate.
